**Source:** youtube.com · [watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0IU5qnr15N8)

### 3. Why AI Agents Are Either the Best or Worst Thing We've Ever Built
**Source:** youtube.com — Hannah Fry · [watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WnzR5aOElvw)

Fry and a friend build an AI agent, hand it a bank card, and watch it open a novelty-mug shop, email a journalist unprompted, and leak their passwords to a stranger. The clearest, least-hyped demo of why "agents that *act*" are a different animal from chatbots.
